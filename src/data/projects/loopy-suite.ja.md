The Loopy Suite is our set of software designed for creating ROM hacks of the Haruhi Suzumiya video games.

## Serial Loops

![Serial Loops Logo](/serial-loops.png)

[Serial Loops](https://haroohie.club/chokuretsu/serial-loops) is the most mature of the suite (and the only one to have a public release). Starting as
a fun side project in 2022 for [Will](https://william278.net) and I, it spawned the entire concept of the Loopy Suite itself and caused us to delve
further into ROM hacking than we ever had previously.

We create releases for virtually every PC platform, including releasing a [Flatpak on Flathub](https://flathub.org/en/apps/club.haroohie.SerialLoops).
Currently, users are capable of editing nearly everything in the game. However, Will and I have even more planned for future releases!
